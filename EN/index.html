Adobe - graphic design and photo retouching

Netfabb & Magics - mesh arrangement

Both programs are used for the arrangement of 3D meshes, in order to correctly carry out the production/printing of said 3D.

Thanks to these powerful tools we can check that there are no holes in the meshes, make the pieces hollow and cut them as required by the piece.

Rhinoceros - 3D Modeling

With this program I carry out most of the mechanical jobs that require greater precision, such as jewelry or pieces that require certain thicknesses and that with other programs, such as ZBrush, is more difficult to control.

In addition to the 3D modeling, I also make use of it to draw outlines and prepare gravure plates, for example for bookmarks or coasters.
